❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MediSphere, our services, and how we support your health journey.

How do I book a consultation with a doctor?+
Visit our Consultations section, select your preferred specialist, and choose an available time slot. You can book video, voice, or in-person appointments. Our system sends instant confirmation and reminders via SMS and email.
Is my medical information secure with MediSphere?+
Absolutely. All your medical data is AES-256 encrypted, stored on ISO-certified servers, and fully compliant with HIPAA and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act. We never sell your data to third parties.
Can I get prescriptions through MediSphere?+
Yes. After a consultation with one of our licensed healthcare providers, you receive a digital prescription that can be filled via our partner pharmacies or at any local pharmacy. We support electronic prescription sharing with 500+ pharmacy networks.
How does the medication tracker work?+
Input your medications, dosages, and schedules. The tracker sends smart reminders at the right times and logs whether you took your dose. You can share adherence reports directly with your doctor for better treatment management.
Do you accept health insurance?+
We work with several major Indian and international insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, and Niva Bupa. Please visit our Insurance page or contact our support team to verify your specific coverage before booking.
Are the health tools clinically validated?+
Yes. Our Symptom Checker, BMI Calculator, and Medication Tracker are built on validated clinical guidelines from WHO, ICMR, and peer-reviewed medical literature. They are tools for guidance — always consult a doctor for a definitive diagnosis.
